Have life balance by keeping a sports routine

Tyron Johnson
10/04/2024
Tyron is a sports guy! He loves to play basket with his sons or run trails along the California coastline.

When I was a dad I stopped all my sports life. Apart of being fatter this decision made me much more stressed and easily triggered.

It is imperative that you keep your hobbies, at a pace suitable for your current situation of course, in order to stay mentally sane.
