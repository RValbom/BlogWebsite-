How to handle insomnia during first 6 months

Mark Doe
13/03/2024
Mark is an enthusiast for skate and snowboard. He is passionate about life and loves to take his son Eric on his hobbies.

The first months of a newborn are really demanding and it's difficult to overcome the long periods without sleep due to all the work or mess that happens every night. It is important to notice how this sleep deprivation affect us along the way and how it can even affect the entire family balance.

Whenever is possible you should get some rest throughout the day. Take advantage of the baby's naps and join him/her to get some much needed sleep. If you have to dismiss the kitchen for a while or if the laundry pile is not getting addressed for today it's okay. There are priorities in life and the biggest should be to get an healthy family dynamic where everyone is taken into account and cared for.
