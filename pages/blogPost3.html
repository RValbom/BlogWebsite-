How to setup a calming sleep routine?

Steve Carell
10/04/2024
Steve is a professional photograph. He loves to capture his daughter personality with a camera.

To be honest, before I implement this routine I was going crazy as my toddler was building a full energy momentum just before we go to bed. She would jump and sing and be all over the place every night until I was mad and yell at her to go to bed immediately. Things got much better as soon as I realize that out routine should be changed.

Firstly you need to understand if, for example, bath time calms your kiddo of boosts his/her energy. In my case, was the later one. So I changed bath time to before dinner. Also, after we have dinner we go to her room and I lower the lights into presence lights mode almost...
